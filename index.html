What is GeekSphere ?

Geeksphere is a tech community initiative that aims to transition from a non-productive pedagogy characterized by rote learning and theoretical assignments to a productive, value-based system.
This system rewards real-world problem-solving, creativity, and collaboration, preparing students not just for exams but for life and career challenges in the rapidly evolving world.
